French Macarons

Description

French macarons are classic meringue-based pastries consisting of almond flour shells filled with various fillings. They are renowned for their delicate textures and diverse flavor profiles. Mastering the technique of making French macarons requires precision and practice. Explore our step-by-step recipe guide to create these iconic treats at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup almond flour, finely ground
  • 1 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 3 egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 drops gel food coloring
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (2 sticks), at room temperature
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream
